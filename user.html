    Before you buy a computer, it's important to identify what you will be using it for and determine your needs for certain parts and specifications. Whether you're a graphic designer, gamer or casual user, there is a device tailored to your needs out there. Each computer has its strengths, weaknesses and suitability.

    There are computers whose specifications make them ideal for gamers depending on their processor speeds and their graphics. For graphic designers, a computer with a large amount of storage, RAM memory and a multiple core processor will be the best option for their use. For those looking for a desktop suitable for their day-to-day use for surfing the internet and saving information, a desktop with a dual-core processor and 1TB storage space is more than enough to suit your needs.

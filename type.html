    Comparing by Type


    When you choose a computer, you want to choose a type that best suits your need with regards to its software capabilities, efficiency, average power, maintenance and portability. There are 3 types of computers that vary with regards to that set of criteria: Macs, PCs and Chromebooks; all of whom have general attributes that make them ideal for different types of users. Here, we'll look at three different computers that fall into each type.

    A Mac is the ideal type of computer for graphic designers, editors or things that take up large amounts of RAM and need high graphics capabilities. Also, Macs are naturally compatible with Adobe softwares that are tailored to the computer making them run more efficiently. PCs are great home computers. With relatively high specifications, workspace compatibility, and large amounts of storage, it is great for casual use, some games and work. Chromebooks are the more light-weight, student-built computers with their capabilities being limited but that doesn't stop them from being light-weight, portable and versatile.
